BBQ Chicken Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • For the salad:
    • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
    • 2 cups water or chicken broth
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
    • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 red bell pepper, diced
    • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1 avocado, sliced or diced
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Olive oil for grilling
  • For the Homemade BBQ Dressing:
    • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

    1. Cook the Quinoa:
      • In a medium saucepan, bring 2 cups of water or chicken broth to a boil.
      • Add the rinsed quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
      • Fluff the quinoa with a fork and set aside to cool slightly.
    2. Prepare the Chicken:
      • Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
      • Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
      • Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked with an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
      • During the last few minutes of grilling, brush each side of the chicken with BBQ sauce.
      • Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into bite-sized pieces.
    3. Assemble the Salad:
      • In a large salad bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, corn, black beans, red bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, and cilantro (if using).
      • Add the grilled chicken pieces to the salad.
    4. Make the BBQ Dressing:
      • In a small bowl, whisk together the BBQ sauce, olive oil, apple cider vinegar or lemon juice, honey or maple syrup (if using), and a pinch of salt and pepper.
    5. Serve:
      • Drizzle the homemade BBQ dressing over the salad and toss everything together until well combined.
      • Top with avocado slices, and garnish with extra cilantro if desired.
      • Serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator for up to 2 hours for a cold salad.
