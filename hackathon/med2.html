Savory Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • For the Stir-Fry:
    • 1 lb (450g) flank steak or sirloin, thinly sliced against the grain
    • 4 cups broccoli florets
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (divided)
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
    • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated (optional)
    • 1/4 cup water (for steaming the broccoli)
    • Cooked white or brown rice, for serving
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
    • 1/4 cup beef broth or water
    • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar or honey
    • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)

    • Instructions:

    1. Prepare the Sauce:
      • In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, beef broth (or water), oyster sauce, cornstarch, brown sugar (or honey), vinegar, sesame oil (if using), and red pepper flakes (if using) until well combined. Set aside.
    2. Cook the Beef:
      • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
      • Add the thinly sliced beef in a single layer and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the beef is browned and cooked through.
      • Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.
    3. Cook the Broccoli:
      • In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
      • Add the minced garlic and grated ginger (if using), and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
      • Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they begin to turn bright green.
      • Pour in 1/4 cup of water, cover the skillet, and steam the broccoli for another 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp.
    4. Combine and Sauce:
      • Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the broccoli.
      • Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and broccoli, stirring to coat everything evenly.
      • Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and everything is heated through.
    5. Serve:
      • Serve the savory beef and broccoli stir-fry over cooked white or brown rice.
      • Garnish with sesame seeds or sliced green onions if desired.
    6. Serve:
      • Drizzle the homemade BBQ dressing over the salad and toss everything together until well combined.
      • Top with avocado slices, and garnish with extra cilantro if desired.
      • Serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator for up to 2 hours for a cold salad.
