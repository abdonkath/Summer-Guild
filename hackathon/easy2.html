Turkey and Veggie Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole-grain or multigrain wraps
  • 8 oz (225 g) sliced turkey breast
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup baby spinach or mixed greens
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green), thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned or shredded
  • 1/4 cup hummus or Greek yogurt (for spreading)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Wash and slice the vegetables as needed.

  2. Assemble the Wraps:
    • Lay each wrap flat on a clean surface.
    • Spread a thin layer of hummus or Greek yogurt evenly over the entire surface of the wrap.
    • Layer with sliced turkey, avocado slices, baby spinach or mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper, and carrot.
  3. Roll the Wraps:
    • Carefully fold in the sides of the wrap, then roll it up from the bottom to the top, making sure to keep the fillings tightly packed.
