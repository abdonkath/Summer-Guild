Thai Curry Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • For the Teriyaki Salmon:
    • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for extra sweetness)
    • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)
    • 1 tablespoon chopped green onions (optional, for garnish)
  • For the Bowl:
    • 2 cups cooked brown rice or quinoa
    • 2 cups steamed or sautéed vegetables (e.g., broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • 1 cup shredded cabbage or mixed greens
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or extra teriyaki sauce (for drizzling)

    • Instructions:

    1. Prepare the Teriyaki Salmon:
      • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) or prepare a grill for medium-high heat.
      • In a small bowl, mix the teriyaki sauce with honey or maple syrup if using.
      • Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil, or on a grill-safe pan.
      • Brush the salmon with olive oil and drizzle with half of the teriyaki sauce mixture.
      • Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or grill for 6-8 minutes per side, until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
      • During the last few minutes of cooking, brush the salmon with the remaining teriyaki sauce mixture.
    2. Assemble the Bowls:
      • Divide the cooked brown rice or quinoa among serving bowls.
      • Arrange the steamed or sautéed vegetables, sliced avocado, and shredded cabbage or mixed greens around the rice.
      • Place a cooked salmon fillet on top of each bowl.
    3. Serve:
      • Drizzle each bowl with a little soy sauce or extra teriyaki sauce if desired.
      • Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if using.
      • Serve immediately and enjoy!
